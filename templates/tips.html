Stay Safe: Tips for Safer Travel

Follow these tips to help keep yourself and others safe while traveling.

  • ✔ Avoid dark or isolated areas when possible.
  • ✔ Share your live location with a friend.
  • ✔ Report any suspicious activity in the app.
  • ✔ Stick to well-lit main roads when walking at night.
  • ✔ Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, change your route.
